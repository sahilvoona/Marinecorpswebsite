Marine Corps League.

West Hudson Detachment 209

Marines, Leathernecks, Jarheads, Devil Dogs…

The Marine House is open to all Marines every Friday evening beginning at 1900.

  • This is NOT your grandfather's Lodge or Legion. Flip through our gallery or yearbook to get an idea of what we do.
  • Join us for one of our monthly meetings. There is no obligation to join.
  • Monthly meetings are held the second Wednesday of each month, 2000 hours.
  • 286 Belgrove Drive Kearny, N.J. 07032

The members of the Marine Corps League join together in camaraderie and fellowship for the purpose of preserving the traditions and promoting the interests of the United States Marine Corps. Those who belong have the respect of a nation, the loyalty of their own and the pride of being a part of history.


Stop by and see what we are all about.
